Thomas Shelby

political,business man

A leader and a powerfull criminal of the organization (peaky blinders) that controls much of birmingham (england), in the 1920s. Born into a modest family, he grows up in birmingham with his brothers and sister.Thomas is a former soldier of world war (1) who suffers a lot from post-traumatic stress, he eventually joinded peaky blinders a local gang known for wearing razor blade caps, he has a strong leadership skills and a strategic thinking that allows him to take control of the gang, with time he became a respected business man and a influancial politician.
